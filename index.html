About Parent.Cc

As a father of three kids under five, I began searching and reading in an effort to be a better-informed parent. I found it very difficult to discover meaningful stuff amidst a lot of noise. As a software engineer I'm accustomed to excellent resources for staying informed, and saw an opportunity to apply what works in my professional field to the discipline of parenting. The result is Parent.Cc, a weekly email summary of news relevant to early-childhood parenting.

Aided by software I've developed for news discovery and curation, I monitor thousands of sources looking for the most meaningful and fascinating stories relevant to parents. I summarize these stories into a weekly email that you can quickly scan to be a better-informed parent.
